YouTube Autoplay Stopper

The YouTube Autoplay Stopper does what it says on the tin: it stops a new video from playing automatically on your YouTube after you finish watching the one you intended to.

⭐ The YouTube Autoplay Stopper works even if you don't have youtube.com in your Nudge sites.

⚠️ Due to YouTube's code changing over time, and different code being shown to different users, the YouTube Autoplay Stopper may not always work.
